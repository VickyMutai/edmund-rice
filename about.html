MISSION

To seek the liberation of people in Langas-Yamumbi, especially of children, youth and women, through respectful engagement with the community, empowering education for a dignified life, advocacy and action for human rights and solidarity.

VISION

Transformed lives of the marginalized and poor children and youth in Langas-Yamumbi for personal and communal growth and development.

CORE VALUES

Principles that govern us include:

  • Education to empower for a dignified life
  • Gospel living: inclusiveness, reconciliation, solidarity and dialogue
  • Prayerful reflection
  • Compassion
  • Community Engagement
  • Advocacy for social justice and human rights
  • Collaboration
